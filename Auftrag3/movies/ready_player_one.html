"Madagascar" is a 2005 animated comedy film produced by DreamWorks Animation. It follows four Central Park Zoo animals—a lion, zebra, giraffe, and hippo—who accidentally find themselves stranded on the island of Madagascar. The film is known for its humor, vibrant animation, and memorable characters like the eccentric lemur King Julien.
