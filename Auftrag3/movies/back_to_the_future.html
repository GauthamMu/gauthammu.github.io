Back to the Future

"Back to the Future" is a 1985 science fiction adventure film directed by Robert Zemeckis. It stars Michael J. Fox as Marty McFly, a teenager who travels back in time to 1955 in a DeLorean car turned time machine, invented by the eccentric Doc Brown. Marty must ensure his parents fall in love to secure his own existence.
