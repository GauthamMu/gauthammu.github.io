Baywatch

"Baywatch" is a 2017 action comedy film directed by Seth Gordon, based on the popular TV series. It stars Dwayne Johnson and Zac Efron as lifeguards who uncover a criminal plot that threatens the future of their beach. The movie combines action, comedy, and plenty of beach-side drama, paying homage to the original series while adding a modern twist.
