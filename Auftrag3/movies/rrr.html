"RRR" is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language period action drama directed by S.S. Rajamouli. It tells a fictional story set in the 1920s about two real-life Indian revolutionaries, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem, and their fight against the British Raj. The film is known for its grand scale, stunning visuals, and powerful performances.
