Fast and the Furious

"Fast and the Furious" is a high-octane action film series that began with the 2001 film directed by Rob Cohen. It follows street racer Dominic Toretto, played by Vin Diesel, and undercover cop Brian O'Conner, played by Paul Walker, as they navigate a world of illegal street racing and heists, emphasizing themes of family and loyalty.
