Home Alone

"Home Alone" is a classic 1990 family comedy directed by Chris Columbus. The film follows 8-year-old Kevin McCallister, played by Macaulay Culkin, who is accidentally left behind when his family goes on vacation. Kevin must defend his home from two bumbling burglars using his wits and an array of booby traps.
